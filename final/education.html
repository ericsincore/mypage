Training/Education Required

The education of a web developer ranges from high school diploma to a bachelor's degre. It is important that as a web develop there is a strong understanding the Hyper Text Markup Language (HTML) programming. A web developer is competitive by knowing other language, such as JavaScript. With career fields in the computer industry it is imperative that the web developer stays up to date with ever changing technology. According to careerkarma the traditional way of entering into this field is through earning a Bachelor's degree. There is also cheaper options to aid in the learning process such as coding bootcamps and the free w3schools website. Additionaly obtaining and maintaing certificates relevant to the industry.
