Salary/Benefits

Salary

Work Hard

According to, Occupational Outlook Handbook: Pay , the median annual wage for a web developer in 2023 was $84,960. The range of pay is quite large, with the bottom 10% earning $46,850 while on the other end the high 10% earned $157,280. Web and digital interface designers on the other hand made slightly more, averaging $98,540.

Benefits

The benefits of working in the web development industry will vary on personal interests and needs. This career allows for remote working, beneficial to those who prefer not going into the office. Projects will vary and prevent the work from becoming mundane, allowing for more personal satisfaction. Some employers will pay for additional education. Working for corporations will provide imporant benefits such as health, dental, and potentially company cars,phones, or computers.
